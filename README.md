# Customer Churn Analytics & Retention Strategy 

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior and identifying high-risk customers using Python, Machine Learning, Power BI, and DAX.

The goal is to understand the main drivers behind customer churn, predict which customers are likely to leave, classify customers into risk segments, and provide actionable retention strategies to support business decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts company revenue and long-term growth.

This project aims to answer the following questions:

* What factors contribute most to customer churn?
* Which customers are at the highest risk of leaving?
* How does contract type affect churn behavior?
* Which payment methods are associated with higher churn rates?
* What retention actions can reduce customer loss?

---

## Dataset

Dataset: Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographic information, subscription details, service usage, billing information, and churn status.

Key features include:

* Customer ID
* Gender
* Senior Citizen
* Partner
* Dependents
* Contract Type
* Internet Service
* Payment Method
* Monthly Charges
* Total Charges
* Tenure
* Churn

---

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Removed unnecessary columns
* Converted data types
* Handled data quality issues
* Encoded categorical variables
* Created tenure groups

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Performed exploratory analysis to identify churn patterns across:

* Contract Types
* Internet Services
* Payment Methods
* Customer Tenure
* Monthly Charges

### Key Findings

* Month-to-month customers have the highest churn rate.
* Electronic Check users are more likely to churn.
* Fiber Optic customers show significantly higher churn.
* New customers are more likely to leave.
* Long-term customers are less likely to churn.

### 3. Machine Learning Model

Model Used:

* Logistic Regression

Steps:

* Train-Test Split
* Model Training
* Prediction
* Performance Evaluation

Model Results:

* Accuracy: ~81%
* Precision, Recall, and F1-Score evaluation performed

### 4. Risk Segmentation

Customers were classified into:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

Using churn probability scores generated by the model.

## Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to move from Month-to-Month contracts to longer-term contracts.
* Create retention campaigns targeting Electronic Check customers.
* Focus customer success efforts on new customers during their first year.
* Prioritize High-Risk customers for proactive engagement.
* Develop personalized offers for customers with high churn probability.
